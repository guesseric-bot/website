Name Origin

'Mezzo' (Italian for 'medium') combined with '-fy' (meaning 'for you')—reflecting our dedication to linking enterprises with patrons through cutting-edge couponing solutions.

Core Purpose

Rather than simply developing digital coupons or applications, Mezzofy positions itself as a pioneer in COUPONS MARKETING infrastructure, making it easy to create, share, redeem, and measure.

Key Objectives

We aim to elevate spirits, forge meaningful connections, unleash creativity, and enable focus, making life more vibrant through the joy of sharing by facilitating interactions between merchants and consumers.
