Mezzofy 助品牌設置優惠券到240 間百佳 NFC 標籤，全程代辦、無需協調

July 21, 2025 • Mezzofy • Blog

Introduction

For many FMCG and consumer goods brands, executing promotional activities through physical retail channels involves substantial coordination and procedural complexity.

Challenges Outlined

The piece identifies typical obstacles: coordinating promotional details with retailers like ParknShop, designing physical coupon materials, arranging logistics and in-store placement, and tracking sales data.

Service Solution

Mezzofy now supports NFC tag promotions across ParknShop's 240 Hong Kong locations, offering a "one-stop" service where brands provide discount content while Mezzofy handles coupon creation, tag setup, and logistics—eliminating direct retailer coordination requirements.

Key Benefits

The platform highlights several advantages:

  • Consumers show 5x higher purchase intent when actively claiming e-coupons
  • Redemption rates reach 30-34% versus traditional paper coupon rates of 15-20%
  • Per-coupon distribution costs fall below HK$0.20, reducing expenses by over 80% compared to physical materials

Target Applications

The service suits new product launches, limited-time promotions, market testing scenarios, and brands avoiding POS or paper-based operations.
