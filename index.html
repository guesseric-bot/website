For Merchants: Turn Coupon Liabilities into High-Performing Digital Assets

Join the Exchange for free and gain instant access to global distribution channels. You only pay when a sale is successfully made and redeemed.

Zero Upfront Cost

Free Onboarding. Pay only a small transaction fee upon final, successful redemption (Performance-Based Model).

Flexible Control

Use our Bridge API with your existing CMS, or utilize our free Mezzofy CMS products. You dictate the rules, we handle the security.

Global Footprint

Instantly expose your inventory to our B2B marketplace, channel partners, and the high-volume NFC Network for physical retail engagement.

Future-Proof Asset

Your promotions are tokenized, opening up possibilities for secondary markets and new financial instruments (RWA Linkage).

For Distributors & Partners: Access the World's Most Secure, High-Intent Coupon Inventory

Enhance your loyalty program, banking app, or corporate HR portal with real-time, verified deals without the financial risk of inventory purchasing.

Risk-Free Integration

Free to Access and seamless integration via the Bridge API. Instantly pull standardized deals into your platform.

Cash Flow Protection

Our Consignment Settlement Model requires only a partial upfront deposit for Large Enterprises, with the bulk of the charge processed only on verified redemption.

Standardized Quality

All coupons adhere to the Mezzofy Coupon Standard, eliminating fraud risk and ensuring a perfect redemption experience for your customers.

Targeted Distribution

Consume inventory tailored for your channels: Loyalty & HR for internal benefits, and the AI Agent API for programmatic consumption.

The Exchange Core: Where Commerce Meets Security

Our Layer 2 Core transforms simple promotional codes into unique, trackable digital assets.

Standardization & Tokenization

Every voucher is tokenized upon ingestion, converting it into a secure, single-use digital asset (NFT/fungible instrument).

Asset Linkage

We create the foundation for linking these assets to Real-World Assets (RWA), expanding the digital coupon market into the rapidly growing Web3/DeFi asset space.

Clearinghouse

The core manages all consignment, financial reconciliation, and fraudless validation.
