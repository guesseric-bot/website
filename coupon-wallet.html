Available Everywhere You Need It

Choose the platform that works best for your customers. Mezzofy Coupon Wallet is accessible across all major platforms and devices.

iOS Mobile App

Native iPhone and iPad experience with seamless Apple Wallet integration and NFC tag support for instant coupon downloads

Android Mobile App

Optimized Android experience with Google Pay integration and NFC capabilities for tap-to-download coupons

Web Application

Access your wallet from any browser, any device. No app installation required for your customers

NFC Physical Card

Physical card with QR code and NFC chip for customers who prefer tangible wallet solutions
