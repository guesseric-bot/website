Mezzofy Partners with Treasure Global to Accelerate Digital Coupon Expansion in Malaysia

April 16, 2025 • Mezzofy • News

Mezzofy announced a strategic partnership with Treasure Global Inc., designating them as the exclusive distributor of its digital coupon platform in Malaysia.

The company positions itself as a regional leader managing over USD40 billion in digital coupon transactions across 15 markets. Its "Coupon-as-a-Service (CaaS)" platform enables businesses to engage customers without requiring new infrastructure investments.

Through this collaboration, Treasure Global will expand platform adoption throughout Malaysia, leveraging their local market expertise and digital capabilities. The partners project achieving "USD2 to 4 million in recurring annual revenue" within the first year.

"Treasure Global's strong presence and market insight make them a great partner to scale our solution in Malaysia."

— Dicky Ying, CEO of Mezzofy

The platform provides features including seamless coupon creation, real-time redemption tracking, and campaign analytics. The partnership reflects Mezzofy's commitment to supporting Malaysian brands in leveraging digital innovation for customer engagement as the nation advances its digital economy.
