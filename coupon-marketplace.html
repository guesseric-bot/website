B2C MARKETPLACE

Business to Consumer

Enable direct-to-consumer coupon distribution through Mezzofy's hosted inventory platform. Businesses can list, manage, and distribute coupons directly to end customers through multiple channels.

  • Direct Distribution: Push coupons to customers via email, SMS, social media, and mobile apps
  • Personalized Campaigns: Target specific customer segments with tailored promotional offers
  • Real-Time Analytics: Track redemption rates, customer engagement, and campaign performance
  • Multi-Channel Reach: Distribute through marketplaces, e-commerce platforms, and retail partners
C2C MARKETPLACE

Consumer to Consumer

Mezzofy enables the first truly secure peer-to-peer coupon exchange. Consumers can transfer, gift, trade, or sell unused coupons to other consumers, creating a secondary market that increases overall redemption rates.

  • Transferable Coupons: Gift or transfer coupons to friends and family with secure validation
  • Secondary Marketplace: Trade or sell unused coupons to maximize value and reduce waste
  • Fraud Prevention: Blockchain-inspired tokenization ensures each coupon is unique and valid
  • Increased Redemption: Unused coupons find new owners, driving actual business value for merchants
