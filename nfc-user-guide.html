Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to download an app to use NFC coupons?

A: No! You can tap and view coupons directly in your mobile browser. However, downloading the Mezzofy Coupon Clipper app provides the best experience with automatic coupon collection and no personal data required.

Q: What if my phone doesn't have NFC?

A: No problem! Every NFC coupon also has a QR code option. Simply open your camera app and scan the QR code to access the same coupon.

Q: Does the Mezzofy Coupon Clipper app require personal information?

A: No! You can start using the app immediately without providing any personal data. It's designed for privacy and convenience.

Q: Can I use a coupon multiple times?

A: It depends on the coupon terms. Most coupons are single-use, but some promotional offers allow multiple redemptions. Check the coupon details for specific information.

Q: What happens if I tap a coupon but don't save it?

A: Without the app, you can still use the coupon immediately from the browser page. You can also save it to your device wallet (Apple, Google, or Samsung) or tap the same NFC sticker again later.

Q: Can I transfer my coupons to someone else?

A: Yes! Through our C2C (Customer-to-Customer) portal, you can trade, gift, or sell unused coupons to other Mezzofy users.

Q: What if the coupon doesn't work at checkout?

A: First, check that the coupon hasn't expired and that you're using it at a participating merchant. If issues persist, contact the merchant or Mezzofy customer support for assistance.

Q: Which wallet should I use to save coupons?

A: We recommend the Mezzofy Coupon Clipper app for the best experience. However, you can also save to Apple Wallet (iPhone), Google Wallet (Android), or Samsung Wallet depending on your device.
